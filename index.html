The Crimson Planet

What is Mars?

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and the second-smallest planet in the Solar System, being larger than only Mercury. In English, Mars carries the name of the Roman god of war and is often referred to as the "Red Planet". It has high radiation, and big dust storms that can last days on end. We belive that Mars may have had water millons of years ago, but think that it was frozen or evaporated.
